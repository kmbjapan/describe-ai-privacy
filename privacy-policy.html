Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2026

DescribeAI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome extension handles your information.

Information We Collect

DescribeAI does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information.

The extension stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • A randomly generated device ID (used solely for daily usage tracking)
  • Daily usage count (resets every 24 hours)

Product Data

When you use DescribeAI, the product name visible on your current browser tab is sent to our secure backend server to generate a product description. This data is used only to process your request and is not stored or logged.

Third-Party Services

DescribeAI uses the OpenAI API to generate product descriptions. Product name data is sent to OpenAI for processing. Please refer to OpenAI's Privacy Policy for details.

Our backend is hosted on Cloudflare Workers. Please refer to Cloudflare's Privacy Policy for details.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with any third parties beyond what is necessary to operate the service (OpenAI and Cloudflare as described above).

Children's Privacy

DescribeAI is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store support page.
